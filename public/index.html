Flip Coin Game

Game Rules

  • Objective : The objective of the game is to make accurate predictions and accumulate coins.
  • Gameplay : - Player begins with five coins.
    - You predict the outcome of a coin flip (heads or tails).
    - If your prediction is correct, you gain 2 coins.
    - After three consecutive correct guesses, you receive an additional 5 coins.
    - If your prediction is incorrect, you lose 2 coins.
    - Clicking the "End Game" button clears all accumulated coins, requiring you to start with the initial 5 coins.
  • HighStake coin : - When you accumulates 10 coins or more, you have the option to stake 10 coins.
    - Successfully guessing the next flip earns the you 25 coins.
    - Failure to guess correctly results in the loss of the staked 10 coins.
  • Winnig : You need to accumulate 40 coins to win the game and earn a Bronze Coin.
  • THE Bronze Coin : The Bronze Coin can be used at any point in the game to increase the your coins by 10.
  • Have Fun : Have fun flipping the coin and see how far you can go in the game!
